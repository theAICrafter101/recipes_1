Biryani Recipe

A flavorful and aromatic rice dish in South Asia, made with basmati rice, tender meat (chicken, mutton, or beef), and a blend of spices, cooked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Tender meat (chicken, mutton, or beef)
  • Blend of spices
  • Yogurt
  • Fried onions
  • Fresh herbs (mint, coriander)
  • Saffron
  • Ghee or oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Marinate the meat with yogurt and spices for at least 2 hours.
  2. Cook the basmati rice until it's 70% done, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large pot, layer the marinated meat and partially cooked rice.
  4. Sprinkle fried onions, fresh herbs, and saffron over the layers.
  5. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for about 45 minutes to an hour.
  6. Once cooked, gently mix the biryani before serving to combine the layers.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad on the side.
